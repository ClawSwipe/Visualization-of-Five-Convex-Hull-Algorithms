What is this?

A interactive map to check if two lines intersect using 3 algorithms, namely the CounterClockwise Method, the Bounding Box method and the Parametric Equation method

A visual representation of Convex Hull algorithms, namely Brute Force, Jarvis March, Graham Scan, Quick Elimination and Monotone Chain. A convex hull will be made around the points you plot.

Go ahead

Click to add nodes. Drag to reposition nodes. Right click on a node to start an edge. Right click on another node to complete the edge. Drag nodes to adjust the weight of the edge. Click buttons to see if your line intersects (maximum 4 nodes). Click the Convex Hull buttons to make a convex hull (any number of nodes you want).
